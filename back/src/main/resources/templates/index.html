DCU Information System

Feeling overwhelmed by university life? Welcome to your one-stop dashboard for navigating campus like a pro! Here, you'll find real-time data on everything from temperature and humidity (perfect for outfit planning) to average student age groups (get ready to mingle!). Plus, we've got you covered on your commute with up-to-date bicycle travel times. But that's not all! This dashboard also gives you the inside scoop on class occupancy levels, so you can snag that perfect seat before heading out. Stay informed, make smart choices, and conquer your university experience - all with a single glance!

This dashboard was created as a group effort of EE417 students, as their group assignment for this module.
